`> system online.`

This is the first entry in the log. The OS is up, the wallpaper has stars on
it, and the menubar clock is ticking. If you're reading this, the build
pipeline survived the trip from MDX to JSX without losing too much.

The plan for this log is small and unromantic: short notes on things that get
built or broken in the open. Project changelogs that don't justify their own
page. Occasional longer pieces when the topic deserves it. No newsletter, no
analytics beyond what Vercel already counts, no comments — if you want to
reply, the contact links are in `PROFILE.dat`.

A few conventions for future entries:

- Titles are uppercased and prefixed with a tag (`BOOT LOG`, `FIELD NOTE`,
  `POSTMORTEM`). Slugs are dash-cased and numbered when there's a series.
- Bodies are MDX, rendered in `iA Writer Mono` at 16px with a 680px max width.
- Dates are real. Drafts live in `draft: true` and never ship to RSS.

`> end log.`
