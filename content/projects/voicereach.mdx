VoiceReach is a mobile AI platform built for homeless outreach workers in
San Francisco. The app uses OpenAI Whisper for real-time transcription and
GPT-4o to automatically structure outreach notes, reducing hours of manual
paperwork and improving reporting consistency across teams.

Built with React Native and FastAPI, the platform supports voice-first data
collection, GPS tagging, consent-based photo capture, and real-time
search/filtering for case management workflows. VoiceReach won 1st place
out of 300+ participants at the SF10x Civics Hackathon and was later
presented on stage at OpenAI DevDay as part of the *Built for SF by SF*
showcase.
