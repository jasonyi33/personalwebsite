Prereq is a real-time AI lecture copilot designed to help students
understand concepts as lectures happen. The system converts live lecture
streams into evolving knowledge graphs, identifies prerequisite gaps, and
generates personalized explanations and study pathways in real time.

Built with Next.js, Express, PostgreSQL, Redis, and WebSocket
infrastructure, the platform achieved sub-500ms latency for live classroom
feedback and adaptive tutoring. The project won 2nd place in the Education
Track at TreeHacks 2026 out of 1000+ participants.
