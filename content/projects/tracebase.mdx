Tracebase is a decentralized compliance platform for AI companies handling
sensitive training and user data. The platform provides immutable
proof-of-consent records using zero-knowledge proofs, Merkle-based
verification, and encrypted off-chain storage through IPFS and AES-256
encryption.

We built developer SDKs in Python and Rust to support high-volume consent
tracking, access revocation, and verifiable audit trails across AI data
pipelines. Tracebase won 1st place out of 400+ teams at the Midnight Global
Hackathon and later entered the Midnight pre-seed accelerator backed by
Input Output Global.
