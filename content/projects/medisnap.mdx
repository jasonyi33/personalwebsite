MediSnap is an augmented reality medical assistant built for Snap Spectacles
to provide hands-free clinical guidance and information retrieval. The
system overlays contextual medical information directly into a clinician's
field of view using OCR, voice interaction, and real-time AR rendering.

The platform was designed to reduce interruptions during patient care and
improve accessibility to procedural information in sterile environments.
Built with Snap Lens Studio and optimized for low-latency performance,
MediSnap won 2nd place in the Snap AR Track at CalHacks 12.0 out of 2000+
participants.
